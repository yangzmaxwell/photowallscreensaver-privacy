本软件是一款完全离线运行的本地屏幕保护程序。我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何个人信息，包括但不限于您的姓名、电子邮件地址、IP地址或您在本地存储的图片内容。

Thank you for using Photo Wall Screensaver (hereinafter referred to as "this software"). We take your privacy protection very seriously.

1. Data Collection

This software is a fully offline local screensaver program. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, including but not limited to your name, email address, IP address, or the content of images stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

To save your personalized settings, this software generates two configuration files (keeper.json and saver.json) locally on your computer. These files store the screensaver activation delay time, image storage location, and image rotation interval. All data remains strictly on your device's hard drive and will never leave your device or be sent to the developer.

3. Third-Party Services & Network Access

This software does not require an internet connection to operate its core functionality. No third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), adware, or data tracking SDKs are integrated into this software.
